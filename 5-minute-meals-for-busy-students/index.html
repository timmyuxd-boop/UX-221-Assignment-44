5-Minute Meals for Busy Students

ByTimbuns March 23, 2026April 6, 2026

Quick, easy meals when you’re short on time

Between classes, assignments, and workouts, you don’t always have time to cook. These meals are fast, simple, and perfect for busy days.

1. Peanut Butter Banana Toast

  • Ingredients: Bread, peanut butter, banana
  • Steps: Toast bread, spread peanut butter, add banana
  • Time: 3–5 minutes

2. Greek Yogurt Bowl

  • Ingredients: Greek yogurt, granola, fruit
  • Steps: Mix everything in a bowl
  • Time: 2 minutes

3. Microwave Scrambled Eggs

  • Ingredients: Eggs, milk (optional)
  • Steps: Mix in a mug, microwave, stir
  • Time: 3 minutes

💡 Tips

  • Keep quick foods stocked (bread, eggs, yogurt)
  • Use the microwave to save time
  • Prep ingredients ahead for even faster meals
